Tectonic Systems

The architecture of Brutahaus relies on the metaphor of lithic partitioning. Unlike traditional fluid UIs that attempt to hide the grid, we expose the seams.

Core Principles

  • High Density
  • Zero Rounding
  • Absolute Contrast
  • Keyboard-First UX

Storage Strategy

Data is persisted to IndexedDB with an optimistic UI flow. Every keystroke triggers a dirty-bit check, ensuring that localStorage is used only as a secondary cache.

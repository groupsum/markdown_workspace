# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We commit to making participation in this project harassment-free for everyone.

## Our standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment include:

- being respectful and constructive
- focusing on technical issues, not people
- accepting feedback gracefully
- prioritizing clarity and collaboration

Examples of unacceptable behavior include:

- harassment, discrimination, or abusive language
- personal attacks
- publishing private information without permission
- disruptive trolling or intimidation

## Enforcement

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing standards of acceptable behavior and may remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, code, wiki edits, issues, and other contributions that are not aligned with this Code of Conduct.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies in all project spaces and when an individual is representing the project in public spaces.
